Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

Few appetizers bring a crowd together quite like warm, ooey-gooey, utterly irresistible Buffalo chicken dip.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 3/4 cup hot sauce
  • 1 1/2 Shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Heat chicken and hot pepper sauce in a skillet over
    heat until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in
    cheese and ranch dressing. Cook and stir until well
    blended and warm, 3 to 5 minutes.

  2. Mix in 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese, and transfer the
    mixture to a slow cooker. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over top.

  3. Cover and cook on Low until dip is hot and bubbly, about 35 minutes.

  4. Serve with celery sticks and crackers.
